The Best No-Code App Builder Just Got a Modern Makeover

Each new month seems to bring another reason to love Softr. Not too long ago, I published a video about their new integration with Notion, and this week, they modernized the builder experience, introduced powerful new features, and launched a fresh new brand.

What's Softr?

Softr let’s you create rich interfaces for the information you organize in spreadsheet and database apps, like Google Sheets and Airtable. With that new integration, Notion became one of the database apps you can use as the backend for your Softr apps. You can manage your information efficiently in your centralized workspace, and then create robust interfaces for others to access the information that’s relevant to them. In my video, I showcased:

  • a membership app;
  • a website that makes it easy to browse recommended products;
  • and a client portal.

The Reimagined Experience

With this week's bevy of updates:

  • Softr gave a sleek, modern look to the building blocks you use to build apps visually. That includes an incredibly useful “Summary” format that’s unlike any database view in Notion.
  • We can now arrange those blocks in various combinations of columns and new tab-based layout that may be my favorite new feature.
  • All of the utility pages that facilitate user accounts, and payments, and other administrative needs have a fresh new look as well.
  • Apps have a fresh new navigation.
