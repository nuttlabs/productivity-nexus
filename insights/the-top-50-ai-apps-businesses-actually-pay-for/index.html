The Top 50 AI Apps Businesses Actually Pay For (with Positive ROI)

Which AI apps do businesses actually subscribe to for a positive ROI? A new report from Andreessen Horowitz reveals the top 50 AI apps by subscription revenue. That's incredibly useful for determining which apps to consider for yourself. What not useful is that static image they used to present the list. So I published the table below with categories, descriptions and links.

A few key takeaways:
‍

  • ‍Horizontal Applications Lead the Way: A majority of spending (60%) is on horizontal applications, which are general-purpose tools that can be used across different departments and industries.
  • ‍Vertical AI Augments, Not Replaces: Most specialized (vertical) AI software is designed to assist and enhance human capabilities, particularly in customer service, sales, and HR, rather than replace workers.
  • ‍"Vibe Coding" is Now a Workplace Trend: Tools that make it easier to build AI applications are gaining traction in business environments, showing a move from consumer hobbyist use to professional application.
  • ‍Consumer Apps are Going Corporate: A large number of AI applications that started as tools for individual consumers are being adapted and adopted for team and enterprise use.
