Instantly Prompt Gemini AI from Your Chrome Address Bar

Prompting Gemini—Google's AI chatbot—is quicker than ever:

  • In your Chrome address bar, type @gemini, a space, then your prompt.
  • Hit return like you'd visit a website, and Chrome will open Gemini with your prompt submitted.

Not working?

The feature is still rolling out to users. If it doesn't work for you, try updating Chrome.

If that doesn't do it:

  1. Visit chrome://flags from your address bar.
  2. Search for Expansion pack for the Site search starter pack.
  3. Enable that flag and relaunch Chrome. 💪
